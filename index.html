Research Work

Advanced Noise Reduction and Feature Enhancement in Medical CT Brain Imaging using Deep Learning

We propose Attn-GAN, a GAN-based model for denoising low-dose CT (LDCT) brain images. It integrates Residual Dense Blocks and Multi-Head Self-Attention in the generator with a PatchGAN discriminator to preserve fine details. Using a hybrid loss (Content, SSIM, Perceptual, Adversarial), Attn-GAN achieves faster convergence, higher PSNR/SSIM, and better diagnostic accuracy than conventional denoising and super-resolution methods.
